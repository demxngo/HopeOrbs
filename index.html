Hope Orbs

Collect the Light, Avoid the Darkness

How to Play

Objective

Collect yellow Hope Orbs while avoiding red enemies. The screen gets brighter as you collect more orbs!

Controls

Move your mouse or finger to control your character

Game Rules

• Yellow orbs = good (collect them)

• Red enemies = bad (avoid them)

• More enemies appear over time
